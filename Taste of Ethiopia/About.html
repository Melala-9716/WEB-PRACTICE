👩‍🍳 Meet Our Injera Master

Name: Almaz Bekele

Almaz has been making injera for over 20 years. She learned the traditional method from her grandmother in a small village in northern Ethiopia. Every morning she carefully prepares the teff batter and cooks injera with patience and precision. For Almaz, injera is more than bread — it is a symbol of Ethiopian culture and family gatherings.

☕Meet Our Coffee Ceremony Specialist

Name – Hana Tesfaye

Hana prepares the traditional Ethiopian coffee ceremony with care and pride. From roasting the beans to serving the rich aroma of fresh coffee, she brings one of Ethiopia’s most cherished traditions to every guest.

🧑‍🍳Meet Our Head Chef

Dawit Mekonnen

Dawit Mekonnen is the heart of our kitchen. With over 12 years of experience in Ethiopian cuisine, he brings passion and creativity to every dish. Growing up in Addis Ababa, Dawit learned traditional cooking techniques from his mother and grandmother. Today, he leads our kitchen team with dedication, ensuring every plate reflects the rich flavors and culture of Ethiopia.
