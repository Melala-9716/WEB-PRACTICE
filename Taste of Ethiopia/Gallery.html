Chef’s Special– Doro Wot

Classic Ethiopian Chicken Stew Slow Cooked in rich berbere sauce and served with fresh injera

  1. Authentic Berbere Spice – bold flavor and deep red color
  2. Slow-Cooked Tender Chicken – melts in your mouth
  3. Caramelized Onion Base – rich and aromatic
  4. Served with Fresh Injera – traditional sourdough flatbread.

Perfect for sharing with family and friends, following traditional Ethiopian dining customs.

Traditional Shared Dining

In Ethiopia, meals are often enjoyed together from a large shared platter. Family and friends gather around, using injera to scoop different dishes, creating a warm and communal experience.
